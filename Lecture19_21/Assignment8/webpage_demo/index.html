Pseudo-Class Demonstration

:hover

Hover over me

Applies when the mouse pointer is over the element.

:focus

Applies when the element has received focus (e.g., via click or tabbing).

:active

Applies when the element is being activated (e.g., a button being pressed).

:nth-child(even)

  • First Item
  • Second Item (Even)
  • Third Item
  • Fourth Item (Even)

Selects elements based on their position among siblings.
