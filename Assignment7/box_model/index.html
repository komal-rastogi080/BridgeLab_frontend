Content
  • Content: The text, images, or other media.
  • Padding: The space *between* the content and the border.
  • Border: The line that goes *around* the padding and content.
  • Margin: The space *outside* the border, separating it from other elements.

box-sizing Demonstration

Both boxes below have width: 300px, padding: 20px, and border: 10px.

box-sizing: content-box (Default)

Total Width: 300px (content) + 40px (padding) + 20px (border) = 360px

box-sizing: border-box

Total Width: 300px. The padding and border are included *inside* the 300px width.
